Listen to Dvořák's piano quintet in A major, Opus 81! It has beautiful melodies in all its four movements.

Gratitudes to the comrades in the piano club, who introduced me to this piece.
2 The advanced piano technique exercises by Hungarian composer Ernő Dohnányi, which features exercises that train you in finger independence, scales, arpeggios, double stops and many more. The complete score is available on IMSLP. Don't practice too hard, though, since the exercises within may strain your fingers. A more elementary set is Hanon, which you should play at least once a day, or whenever you have the time to practice if you are a hobby pianist like me.
